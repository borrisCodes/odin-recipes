Apple Pie Recipe

An apple pie is a fruit pie in which the principal ingredient is apples. pple pie is often served with whipped cream, ice cream, custard or cheddar cheese.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium sliced and peeled apples
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 3/4 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tbsp salt
  • 1/8 tbsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425 F. Place 1 pie crust in ungreased 9-inch glass pie plate
  2. Gently mix filling ingredients and top with second crust. Cut slits or shapes in the top crust
  3. Bake 30 to 45 minutes or until apples are tender and crust is golden brown
