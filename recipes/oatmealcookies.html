Brown Sugar Oatmeal Cookies Recipe

Soft and chewy. One bowl. No raisins.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups packed dark brown sugar
  • 1 cup salted butter, softened
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3 cups old-fashioned oats

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Beat the brown sugar and butter in an electic mixer, then add vanilla and eggs one at a time
  3. Mix the flour, salt, and baking soda in a meidum bowl
  4. Use a cookie scoop to drop dough onto the baking sheet and bake for 12 to 13 minutes
  5. Let the cookies cool, then transfer to a plate for serving
